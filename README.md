# Capital International School

Capital International School is a CBSE school in Rampura, North Bangalore, offering education from Kindergarten to Grade 10. The school focuses on academics, STEM learning, smart classrooms, sports, swimming, student safety, and transport support.

Capital International School provides a child-friendly learning environment for students from Kindergarten to Grade 10, with a focus on CBSE academics and practical learning.
